Pineapple Smoothie

Description

Smoothies are a convenient and delicious way to incorporate a variety of fruits into your daily diet. This pineapple smoothie recipe is no exception, as it combines the tropical flavors of pineapple and banana with the creaminess of yogurt and the sweetness of orange juice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of ice
  • 2 cups Pineapple chunk
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 2 cups pineapple or orange juice
  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 2 Tablespoons honey

Steps

  1. Add ice, pineapple, banana, pineapple juice, yogurt, and honey into blender
  2. Pulse until it is smooth and creamy to your liking. Serve immediately
