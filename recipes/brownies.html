Classic Brownies

Description

Who doesn't love

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of ice
  • 2 cups Pineapple chunk
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 2 cups pineapple or orange juice
  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 2 Tablespoons honey

Steps

  1. Add ice, pineapple, banana, pineapple juice, yogurt, and honey into blender
  2. Pulse until it is smooth and creamy to your liking. Serve immediately
